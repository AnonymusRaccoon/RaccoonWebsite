Eau

Eau is a launcher that allow you to start games without carring of witch launcher you need to start.

Download See the web version

Gather all your games together

The app gather every game you own in the sameplace.
For now, only Steam is supported

Recently played games

The app show you what you played recently. Gain time and stop searching for a game everytime you want to play.

Connect to launchers and the app will remember you.

The app will use cookies (if you use the web interface) or use a local file to remember you.
